Travel to Learn to Freedive

If you're intrigued by the thought of swimming with the fauna of the sea without the help of heavy equipment, freediving may be just the thing for you to try on your next warm-weather vacation!

Top Freediving Destinations

  • Egypt
  • Greece
  • Indonesia
  • Bahamas

There are still some equipment vital to making your freediving experience enjoyable and safe. Most of the things on this list can be rented at the freediving school of your choice.

Packing List

  • Wetsuit
  • Fins
  • Fin Booties
  • Diving Mask
  • Dive Computer
  • Snorkel
  • Sunscreen
  • Weight Belt and Weights
